ADDITIONAL ATTRIBUTION:

This software implements algorithms inspired by:
- Jacques Monod's work on enzyme kinetics and allostery
- Archibald Hill's equation for cooperative binding (1910)
- Modern optimization techniques via SciPy

Scientific references:
- Monod, J., Wyman, J., & Changeux, J. P. (1965). "On the nature of allosteric
  transitions: A plausible model." Journal of molecular biology, 12(1), 88-118.
- Hill, A. V. (1910). "The possible effects of the aggregation of the molecules
  of haemoglobin on its dissociation curves." The Journal of Physiology, 40, iv-vii.
